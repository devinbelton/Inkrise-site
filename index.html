What is Inkrise?

Think of a story not as a line, but as a tree. Authors write chapters and seed new branches. Readers vote on the chapter that continues the main path—while other branches remain open for exploration. Great ideas rise together.

How it works

1) Seed

Start a new story or add a fresh branch to an existing chapter.

2) Vote

Readers vote within set windows. The winning branch becomes canon.

3) Rise

Worlds grow. Side paths remain explorable for “what-ifs” and deep dives.

FAQ

Can anyone start a story?

Yes. During beta, you can seed new stories or branch existing chapters (respecting content guidelines).

How do votes work?

Each story has voting windows. The top branch is promoted as canon; other branches remain available to explore.

Can I collaborate?

Yes. Invite co-authors to draft, branch, and polish chapters together.
