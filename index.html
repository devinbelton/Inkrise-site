What is Inkrise?

Think of a story not as a line, but as a tree. Authors write chapters and seed new branches. Readers vote on the chapter that continues the main path—while other branches remain open for exploration. Great ideas rise together.

Features

Branching Stories

Every chapter can sprout new paths—stories evolve organically.

Reader Votes

Readers decide what rises next by voting on active branches.

Collaborative Worldbuilding

Writers and readers build worlds together through interaction.

FAQ

Can anyone start a story?

Yes. You can seed a new story or branch an existing chapter (while respecting content guidelines).

How do votes work?

Each story has voting windows. The top branch continues; others remain open for exploration.
