About Inkrise

Inkrise is a platform for collaborative storytelling. Multiple writers can join, add branches, and let the readers vote on where the story should go next. It's unpredictable, creative, and community-driven.

Features

Branching Stories

Start a story and let other writers take it in new directions.

Community Voting

Readers decide which branch becomes canon by voting.

Creator Dashboard

Track your reads, votes, tips, and subscriptions in one place.

FAQ

Is Inkrise free to use?

Yes! You can read and participate for free. Premium features may come later.

Can I start my own story?

Absolutely. Any member can start a new story and invite others to branch.

How do votes work?

Readers vote on their favorite branches. The winning branch continues the story.
