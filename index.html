What is Inkrise?

Think of a story not as a line, but a tree. Authors write chapters and seed new branches. Readers vote on the chapter that continues the main path—while other branches remain open for exploration. Great ideas rise together.

How it works

1. Seed

Create a new story or extend an existing one with a branching chapter.

2. Vote

Readers vote during set windows. The highest-voted branch becomes canon.

3. Explore

Unchosen branches remain as “what-ifs” for readers to wander and enjoy.

Features

  • Branching editor — Write and fork chapters with ease.
  • Reader votes — Timeboxed voting keeps the story moving.
  • Canon & “what-ifs” — The top branch continues; the rest live on.
  • Creator pages — Showcase your stories and grow your audience.
  • Clean, accessible UI — Built for phones first.

FAQ

Can anyone start a story?

Yes. You can seed a new story or branch an existing chapter (respecting content guidelines).

How do votes work?

Each story has voting windows. The top branch continues; others remain explore-able forks.

Is there moderation?

Yes—community guidelines, reporting, and creator controls keep things healthy.
