What is Inkrise?

Think of a story not as a line, but as a tree. Authors write chapters and seed new branches. Readers vote on the chapter that continues the main path‚Äîwhile other branches remain open for exploration. Great ideas rise together.

How it works

1. Seed

Start a world or a chapter. Set the tone, genre, and rules of the story.

2. Branch

Authors propose next-chapters. Each proposal becomes a branch on the story tree.

3. Vote

Readers vote on what rises. The winning branch becomes canon; others remain explorable.
