What is Inkrise?

Think of a story not as a line, but as a tree. Authors write chapters and seed new branches. Readers vote on the chapter that continues the main path—while other branches remain open for exploration. Great ideas rise together.

How it works

1) Seed

Start a new tale or continue one you love. Short chapters keep momentum.

2) Branch

Offer alternate chapters. Each choice creates a new path for the story.

3) Vote

Readers vote in timed windows. The top branch becomes canon; others stay explorable.

FAQ

Can anyone start a story?

Yes. You can seed a new story or branch an existing chapter (respecting content guidelines).

How do votes work?

Each story has voting windows. The top branch continues; others remain explore-able forks.

Is there moderation?

Yes. Community guidelines and reporting keep collaboration healthy and fun.
